    Who am I?

    I’m Genne Pasco, a dedicated student of Hotel and Restaurant Management (HRM) with a passion for hospitality, business, and the exciting world of property ownership. This website is a reflection of my journey from being an HRM student to exploring the entrepreneurial path of building ownership and management. Through this platform, I aim to share insights, experiences, and the knowledge I’ve gained about managing properties, designing spaces, and creating businesses that thrive. As a future hotel and restaurant manager, I believe that understanding the dynamics of building ownership is crucial to success in the hospitality industry.

    Mission | Vision

    ☑️ To inspire and guide aspiring hospitality managers and entrepreneurs in exploring the world of building ownership, offering practical tips and insights into property management and business success.
    ☑️ To create a resourceful platform where students and young professionals can gain the knowledge and confidence to pursue building ownership and management, particularly within the hospitality industry.
